Kevin Devine

Hey, I’m an Irish designer and facilitator who loves to work with people. Wether that’s helping businesses solve complex problems or at the coalface with developers to help deliver great products, once you get the right crew it makes the work enjoyable.

I’ve been lucky enough to work on some excellent projects with really talented people over the years. I’m pretty proud of my work on the Irish Covid app and my time in Ryanair where I led the design on their first native apps.

Since then, I’ve been really enjoying the Discovery process, helping to mold our approach in NearForm and with our clients.

I’ve squeezed in a few talks and love to catch up with the Irish design community when possible.

Away from the desk, I’m into all types of sports and have recently got into running and coaching my kids gaelic football team. I still think I can play basketball and football but my hamstrings have other ideas.

You can find a flavour of my work here and I’m always up for a coffee
